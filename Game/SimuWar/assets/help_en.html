About

This application is a real time war simulation game.

You move your army's units, and wipe out enemies. Then you will conquer a stage.

The operations are simple. You drag the "Move Marker". Your units move to marker's places.

"Move Marker"

Units have differing abilities. You efficiently move units.

And units have some skills. You should use that properly.

You can clear a stage in about 1-5 minutes. "Clear Time" and "Score" are recorded. You must aim at the high score.


Masakazu Yanai designed and developed this game.

Control

Normal View (Select Destination)

In the "Normal View", you drag the "Move Marker". Your units automatically move to marker's places.

"Move Marker"

The move power is different in each unit.

The cavalryman etc. move quickly. But the bowman and the magician, etc. is slow.


If you want to drag the "Move Marker", you touch the "Move Button" that is located in the upper left portion of the screen.

"Move Button"

If You touch the "Range Button" that is located in the upper left portion of the screen, you can drag some "Move Marker".

"Range Button"


Unit Information View (Select Skills)

If You touch the "Unit Information Button", screen is changed to the "Unit Information View"

"Unit Button"

In the "Unit View", you touch a unit, and you show a unit information.

If you touch your unit, you can select a skill.

In the "Unit Information View", the timer stops.



Option View

If you touch the "MENU" key on the device, screen is changed to the "Option View".

In the "Option View", you can save data, you can go to the "Title View".

And you can set sounds, difficult, view messages, and othes.



Drag the Map

If you select a "Move Button" and you drag the map, you can move the map.

If you drag the small map that is located in the upper right portion of the screen, you can move the map.



Timer

The timer is located in the upper left portion of the screen. The number of timer is a elapsed time.

You must aim at the shortest time.

Character

Main Character
"Shell Pink"

She was born into a distinguished noble family. She is the oldest child.

She is a rough neck. She was sent to the boarding war college for her misconduct.

But she enjoys the war college.

She want to be the first female admiral.



Teacher
"Frosty Gray"

She was a rare female captain in the kingdom. She was retired. She is an instructor now.

She is having a hard time with Shell Pink. She looks gloomy every time.

She is an unhappy woman. She is a loser with male. She is a women remaining single in their 30's.
